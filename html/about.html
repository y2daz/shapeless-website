About

Dr. Dharshana Kasthurirathna - Supervisor

A senior lecturer in the Faculty of Computing, Dr. Dharshana graduated from the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, University of Moratuwa. He attained his Masters degree in Computer Science from the University of Colombo. With over 7 years of experience as a Software Engineer and a Researcher in the ICT industry, he obtained his PhD in Complex Self-Organising Systems from the University of Sydney in 2016. His research interests include Distributed Systems, Machine Learning, Self-organising networks, Computational Game theory and Social Network Analysis.
Dr. Dharshana supervised the group "shapeless", guiding them and helping immensely in the development of their product.
